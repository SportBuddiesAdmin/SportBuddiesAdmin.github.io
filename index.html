Connect with Sports Buddies and do sports together!

Ever lacked the numbers to play a friendly match of Soccer or Basketball? This app connects users and gathers players for a match in any form of sport. With wide coverage of sporting locations throughout Singapore, the app caters to a variety of sports such as Soccer, Badminton and Basketball. The SportBuddies App provides extensive functionalities such as in-app messaging with other users and a Buddies Network.


The SportBuddies App is an app that connects users and gathers players for a match in any form of sport. The app provides wide coverage of sporting locations throughout Singapore. This app allows users to book slots to the various sport locations corresponding to the timings that they would like to play sport at any particular location. Users are able to view the slots booked by other users and will be able to join in the fun by placing a booking in the same slot. This allows users to connect via their telegram handles provided and come together for a friendly match. Location reviews are also included to enhance the quality of user experience. Organization of matches are made seamless with our platform.
